Project Vanilla Plus

Vanilla Plus is a closed project and is being developed by XxPoggyisLitxX. The project tries to expand on the survival aspects of vanilla Minecraft.

What does it have?

This project has a lot of goals it needs to accomplish. One of those goals needs to force you as the player to go out and adventure the new fresh world.

You would Notice Imediatly that all biomes have been upgraded to have more structures and potential for the player to explore and obtain treasure.
